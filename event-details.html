Technology

Annual Tech Symposium 2025

April 25, 2025 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM Main Auditorium By Computer Science Society

About This Event

Join us for the Annual Tech Symposium 2025, the premier technology event on campus that brings together students, faculty, industry professionals, and technology enthusiasts.

This year's symposium will feature cutting-edge presentations, interactive workshops, networking opportunities, and demonstrations of emerging technologies. Whether you're a computer science major, a tech hobbyist, or simply curious about the latest innovations, this event offers something for everyone.

The symposium will cover a wide range of topics including:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Blockchain and Cryptocurrency
  • Cybersecurity and Digital Privacy
  • Web3 and Decentralized Applications
  • Augmented and Virtual Reality
  • Quantum Computing
