About

The NEUB CSE Society is the premier student organization dedicated to promoting computer science education, research, and professional development at North East University Bangladesh. We foster a vibrant community where students can explore cutting-edge technologies, develop practical skills, and build networks with industry professionals.

Founded in 2018, our society has grown to become one of the largest student bodies on campus, organizing workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, tech talks, and career development programs. We are committed to bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, helping students prepare for successful careers in technology.

Operating under the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at NEUB, we work closely with faculty members who provide mentorship and guidance for our initiatives. Our programs are designed to complement the academic curriculum and provide hands-on experience with emerging technologies.
