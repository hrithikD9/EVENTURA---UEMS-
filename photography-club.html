About

The Photography Club is a creative space for photography enthusiasts to share knowledge, organize photo walks, and showcase their work. We welcome photographers of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced, who are interested in exploring the art of visual storytelling.

Our club activities include regular photo walks around the campus and city, workshops on various photography techniques, guest lectures from professional photographers, and seasonal exhibitions showcasing our members' best work. We also organize competitions and collaborate with other creative clubs for interdisciplinary projects.
