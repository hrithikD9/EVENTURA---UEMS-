About Eventura

Building a thriving campus community through seamless event management and organization collaboration

Our Mission

Eventura was founded with a clear mission: to transform how university communities connect, engage, and grow through events and organizations. We believe that meaningful campus experiences happen when barriers to discovery and participation are removed.

Our platform bridges the gap between students seeking enriching experiences and organizations looking to expand their reach and impact. By centralizing event management, organization discovery, and attendee engagement, we empower everyone involved in campus life to focus on what matters—building connections and creating memorable experiences.

At Eventura, we're committed to fostering an inclusive environment where all campus voices can be heard and all interests can find their community.

Our Story

Eventura began as a student project at NEUB in 2023, born from a frustration that too many campus events went undiscovered and too many student organizations struggled to reach potential members.

Three Computer Science students—Atqiya, Hrithik, and Mehedi—recognized the disconnect between amazing campus opportunities and the students who would benefit from them. After surveying hundreds of peers, they developed the first version of Eventura for their university.

What started as a solution for one campus quickly expanded as neighboring universities expressed interest. Today, Eventura serves dozens of educational institutions, helping students discover their communities and organizations thrive in their missions.

Our Journey

How Eventura evolved from a class project to a comprehensive campus engagement platform

January 2023

Initial Concept

Three NEUB CS students identified the need for better campus event management systems and began conceptualizing Eventura.

March 2023

First Prototype

Development of the initial prototype began, focusing on core event listing and registration features.

June 2023

Beta Launch

Beta version launched at NEUB with 20 participating student organizations and over 500 early adopters.

September 2023

Organization Features

Added comprehensive organization management tools, including member management and notification systems.

January 2024

Multi-Campus Expansion

Expanded to 5 additional universities with customized experiences for each campus community.

April 2024

Analytics Launch

Introduced comprehensive analytics dashboard for organizations and administrators.

April 2025

Where We Are Today

Now serving 25+ campuses with a community of 50,000+ active users and over 250 organizations.
