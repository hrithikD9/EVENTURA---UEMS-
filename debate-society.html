About

The Debate Society is dedicated to fostering critical thinking and public speaking skills through competitive debates and regular discussions. We provide a platform for students to develop persuasive arguments, analytical abilities, and confidence in articulating their views on diverse topics.

Our society hosts internal debates, participates in regional and national competitions, and organizes workshops on rhetoric, argumentation, and public speaking. Members gain valuable skills in research, logical reasoning, and persuasion that are applicable across academic disciplines and future careers.

April 21, 2025

Registration Open for National Debate Championship

We are excited to announce that registration for the National Collegiate Debate Championship is now open. All interested members should submit their applications by April 30th. Selection will be based on past performance and a tryout round.

Guest Speaker Series: Supreme Court Justice

We are honored to host Supreme Court Justice Eleanor Richards for a special lecture on "The Art of Legal Argumentation" on May 10th. This is a rare opportunity to learn from one of the country's top legal minds.

New Debate Format Introduction

Starting next month, we will be introducing the British Parliamentary debate format for our internal competitions. A training workshop will be held on April 28th to familiarize all members with the rules and strategies.
