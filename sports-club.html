About

The Sports Club is dedicated to promoting physical fitness, sportsmanship, and team building through a variety of athletic activities on campus. Our club offers numerous sporting disciplines including basketball, volleyball, soccer, tennis, and track & field, catering to athletes of all skill levels.

We organize regular training sessions, intramural competitions, and represent the university in regional and national collegiate tournaments. The club also hosts fitness workshops, wellness events, and recreational activities designed to promote an active and healthy lifestyle among the university community.
